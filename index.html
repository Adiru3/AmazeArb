Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have access to my crypto or funds?

Absolutely not. AmazeArb heavily encrypts your Exchange API keys upon setup. Furthermore, the bot only functions with 'Trade' permissions. If you enable 'Withdrawal' permissions, the bot will warn you and reject the configuration entirely.

How do P2P bypasses work?

High-volume P2P exchanges utilize anti-scraping mechanics to mask raw Fiat premiums. AmazeArb integrates sophisticated request routing architectures that safely interact with these endpoints like a genuine browser would, ensuring no latency during scraping.

License provisions are fully automated through the Telegram bot interfacing with CryptoPay (USDT, TON) and native Telegram Stars. Upon payment, your Telegram ID is instantly provisioned and verified across the network.
