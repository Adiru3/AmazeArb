At AmazeArb, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. Our architecture is designed to be "Trustless" - meaning you don't need to trust us with your data, because we never see it.

1. Local-First Architecture Unlike cloud bots, AmazeArb runs 100% locally on your machine. Your trading data, strategies, and logs exist only on your hard drive.
2. API Key Encryption Your API keys are encrypted immediately upon entry using military-grade AES-256 encryption. They are stored in a local database (`data.db`) and are only decrypted in your system's RAM during active trading execution.

  • Telegram API (to send you notifications)
